Your questions answered

What types of fund managers use Nexus for fund administration?
A wide range of investors rely on Nexus, including institutional funds, solo GPs, and established venture capital firms. Our scalable fund administration solutions support investment vehicles like Venture Funds, SPVs, Rolling Funds, and Scout Funds.
How does Nexus support funds raising institutional capital?
Nexus supports venture capital funds raising institutional capital with a full-stack, white-glove approach. We provide dedicated account management, an institutional investor relations team, audit support, and quarterly financial statements.
How does Nexus simplify venture capital operations?
Rather than pairing piecemeal spreadsheets and service providers, Nexus centralizes operations across the fund lifecycle — from marketing your fund through a branded data room, to initiating capital calls, managing your portfolio, and downloading accountant-verified financial statements.
What makes Nexus a top fund administrator for venture capital?
Nexus offers tech-enabled, premium service for funds and syndicates. Our fund administration infrastructure flexibly supports a range of VC firms — from all-in-one fund management for solo GPs to dedicated, white-glove support for institutional firms and their investors.
How does Nexus help emerging managers with full-stack launch support?
For many emerging managers, Nexus can enable funds to launch in as little as 2 weeks with full-stack support. This structure reduces legal costs, speeds time to launch, and eliminates the need to build a back office from scratch.
