FAQs

Common pricing questions

Does Nexus support fund migrations from other fund administrators?

Yes — our onboarding team handles full profile and data migrations. The process typically takes 2–5 business days depending on data volume and fund complexity.

Is there a setup fee for launching a Venture Fund?

There is a one-time implementation fee based on fund complexity. Our team will assess your setup during the onboarding call and provide a transparent quote before you commit.

Is there a charge based on the fund size?

Yes — both plans include a percentage of fund size component in the annual fee (0.1% for Institutional, 0.15% for Full Service), plus a flat annual base. There are no hidden per-transaction fees.

What add-on services are available for Venture Funds?

Add-ons include fund taxes, quarterly financial statements, audit support, blocker entities, parallel fund structures, and advanced reporting. Contact our team for a full list and pricing.

What other fund structures does Nexus support?

Beyond standard venture funds, Nexus supports Rolling Funds, Scout Funds, SPVs, Roll-Up Vehicles, and platform-advised funds. For complex or bespoke structures, contact our team.

FAQs

Common pricing questions

Does Nexus differ from other SPV administrators?

Nexus combines modern software with expert service, offering a flat $8k fee that includes SPV administration, investor closings, distributions, and transaction review — all in one transparent price.

How much does it cost to run an SPV?

Our flat fee is $8k per SPV, which includes administration, investor closings, distributions, and transaction review & execution. Total SPV fees are capped at 10% of the raised amount, excluding add-on service fees.

Are follow-on SPVs the same price?

Yes — each SPV is priced at the same flat $8k rate. There are no volume discounts, but the simplicity means you always know your costs upfront.

What is the minimum raise for SPVs on Nexus?

Since total SPV fees are capped at 10% of the raised amount and our base fee is $8k, the practical minimum raise is around $80k. We recommend SPVs of $250k+ for optimal cost efficiency.

What is the minimum I personally have to invest in each deal?

There is no minimum personal investment requirement. You can invest any amount you're comfortable with, subject to the deal's minimum investment threshold set by the company.
