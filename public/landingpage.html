• FAQs

Common pricing questions

Does Nexus support migrations from other networking platforms?
Yes — our onboarding team handles full profile migrations from AngelList, LinkedIn, and Gust. The process typically takes 2–5 business days depending on data volume.
Is there a setup fee for launching a profile?
Basic profiles on Nexus are free to create and explore. Premium features — including deal pipeline tracking, document vault, and advanced matching — are available on paid plans.
Is there a charge based on deal size or investment amount?
No. Nexus charges flat subscription fees based on your plan tier. We do not take a percentage of any deal, investment, or transaction facilitated through the platform.
What collaboration tools are available for Investors?
Investors get access to formal collaboration requests, deal pipeline management, portfolio tracking dashboards, document access controls, real-time messaging, video meeting rooms, and calendar scheduling — all within a single interface.
What other fund structures does Nexus support?
Beyond standard venture funds, Nexus supports Rolling Funds, Scout Funds, SPVs (Special Purpose Vehicles), Roll-Up Vehicles, and digital subscription-based funds. Contact our team for complex or bespoke structures.
